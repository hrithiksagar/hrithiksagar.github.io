About

Building AI that understands documents like humans do.

I'm an MS by Research student at IIIT Hyderabad, working on Vision-Language Models, Multimodal Document Understanding, and Indic AI — the frontier of making AI systems that can read, reason about, and ground answers in complex documents.

At BharatGen, I contributed to Patram-7B — India's first foundational VLM for documents, released on HuggingFace and the IndiaAI AIKosh platform. My research spans grounded chain-of-thought reasoning, SAM-based segmentation, and SFT/DPO training pipelines.

I hold a B.Tech in CSE (AI & ML) from Sharda University, where I graduated with a 8.4 GPA on a full tuition scholarship for academic excellence.
